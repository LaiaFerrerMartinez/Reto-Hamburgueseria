OFFBOARDING

TERMINATION OF THE CONTRACT

Disciplinary Dismissal

Rights:

  • Right to challenge it before the labor court for inadmissibility or nullity.
  • Right to reinstatement and procedural wages if the contract is declared null and void.
  • 33 days’ salary per year worked (capped at 24 months) if the dismissal is ruled unfair.

Dismissal

Rights:

  • 15 days' notice or equivalent compensation.
  • 20 days' compensation for each year worked.
  • Right to request reinstatement or judicial compensation.

Collective Dismissal

Rights:

  • Consultation period with representatives (minimum 30 days).
  • Compensation of 20 days' salary per year worked (maximum 12 monthly payments).
  • Rehire priority for 6 months after the end of the collective dismissal.

WE LOOK AFTER YOUR HEALTH

OCCUPATIONAL RISK PREVENTION

  • Mandatory PPE: non-slip footwear, nitrile gloves, apron, hat/hairnet, and earplugs depending on the task.
  • Hygiene and order: constant cleaning of surfaces, FIFO food rotation, and safe storage of chemicals.
  • Ergonomics: countertops at appropriate height, use of carts for heavy loads, and active breaks every 2 hours.
  • Signage and cleaning: "wet floor" signs and spill cleanup protocols to prevent slipping.
  • First aid and evacuation: accessible first aid kit, CPR-trained employees, and biannual evacuation drills.
