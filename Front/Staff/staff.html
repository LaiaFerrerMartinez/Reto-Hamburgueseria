Work Shifts 2025:

At Redwood Grill, we work with a six-shift system that guarantees 40 hours per week, with Mondays closed and one rotating day off between Tuesday and Sunday.

Each employee is assigned to one of these shifts and follows the same pattern throughout the year, with split shifts from 12:00–16:00 and 20:00–00:00. If a holiday falls within their workweek, the employee may enjoy an additional day of rest increased by 50% (equivalent to 12 extra hours).

WORKING DAY

Each worker will enjoy a maximum work week of 40 hours, distributed in 8-hour shifts, with at least one full day of rest each week .

INTERNAL NEGOTIATION PROPOSALS

Team initiatives to improve schedules, incentives, and well-being:

Flexibility in “A la Carta” Vacation Scheduling

Option to swap weekends: You and a colleague choose which weekend you want off, note it on the shared shift sheet, and HR verifies coverage.
Once approved, the swap is reflected in the calendar and counts as a regular shift.

Employee Referral Bonus Program: “Refer a Friend”

Receive €50 net for each hiring referral that lasts at least 3 months: build trust and reduce turnover. Simply nominate the candidate to HR before their interview.

Me-Time Days & Family Discount

Me-Time Day: One day off per year (full shift) for personal rest, with a 7-day notice.
Family Discount: 30% off meals for family members during off-peak hours (weekdays, before 4:00 PM).

ANNUAL LEAVE

Each employee will be entitled to paid annual leave of 30 calendar days or 22 working days, to be taken in two periods. The vacation schedule will be set by mutual agreement between the company and the employee, with notice given at least two months in advance of the main period. If part of the vacation time coincides with paid holidays, these will not be deducted from the rest day calculation.

SALARY COMPENSATION

The worker will receive a total of 14 annual payments, distributed in 12 regular monthly payments plus 2 extraordinary payments, which will be paid in June and December.
